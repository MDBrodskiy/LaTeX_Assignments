\begin{itemize}

  \item Claim $-$ A statement that answers the original question

    \begin{center} What do you know? \end{center}

  \item Evidence $-$ Scientific data that supports your claim. Tells the audience the support you have collected that makes the claim convincing.

    \begin{center} How do you know that? \end{center}

  \item Reasoning $-$ Reasoning that involves a rule or scientific principle that describes why the evidence supports the claim

    \begin{center} Why does your evidence make sense? \end{center}

\end{itemize}
